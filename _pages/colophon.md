This website first showed up on the internet in the spring of 2013, published from Tampa, Florida.

It ran on [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/), and was hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://docs.github.com/en/pages). At the time it mostly functioned as my apprenticeship blog while I was learning how to be a software developer.

Since then it's gone through a frankly irresponsible number of versions. A few of them are gone forever (victims of time, questionable git practices, and the occasional "this seemed like a good idea at 2 AM" rewrite).

The current iteration is open source and lives on [GitHub](https://github.com/meaganewaller/website).
